Nepal Tax Compare Features & Guide

A comprehensive overview of the progressive tax laws, deductions, credits, and the features built into this application.

layers

FY 2083/84 Unified Slabs

The budget for FY 2083/84 has completely replaced the separate Single vs. Married tax tables with a single, unified progressive schedule for all resident individuals, significantly increasing the tax-free limit.

  • Up to NPR 1,000,000: 1% Social Security Tax (SST)
  • NPR 1,000,001 - 1,500,000: 10% Income Tax
  • NPR 1,500,001 - 2,500,000: 20% Income Tax
  • NPR 2,500,001 - 4,000,000: 27% Income Tax
  • Above NPR 4,000,000: 29% Income Tax (Reduced from 39%)
shield

Retirement Deductions (Cap: 500k)

Employees contributing to approved retirement funds can deduct their contributions from their taxable income to lower tax liability.

  • Maximum Deduction: Lower of actual contributions, 1/3 of assessable income, or **NPR 500,000** annually.
  • SSF Contributions: Employee 11% + Employer 20% (total 31% basic salary). In this app, employer contributions are added to assessable income and included in the deduction cap calculations.
  • EPF Contributions: Employee 10% + Employer 10% basic salary.
  • CIT Contribution: Employees can voluntarily add Citizen Investment Trust contributions.
volunteer_activism

1% SST Waiver for SSF

Salaried individuals contributing to the Social Security Fund (SSF) are eligible for a complete waiver of the 1% Social Security Tax on the first income slab.

  • SSF Contributors: Pay **0%** on the first NPR 1,000,000 of taxable income in FY 2083/84 (or first 500k/600k in FY 2082/83).
  • Non-SSF Contributors (EPF/CIT/None): Pay **1%** on the first slab.
paid

Flex Bonus Distribution

Decide how your annual bonus, gratuity, or other occasional incomes affect your monthly budget. Toggle between two modes:

  • Distribute Bonus Monthly (ON): Spreads the net bonus and its marginal tax liability evenly across 12 months for a balanced, regular monthly take-home display.
  • Keep Bonus Separate (OFF): Excludes the bonus cash and its associated marginal tax from regular monthly cash-in-hand. Net monthly values reflect base pay, and the bonus is shown as a separate payout: (+Rs. XXX bonus when taken).
card_membership

Tax Exemptions & Credits

Full support for other deductions and rebates permitted under the Nepal Income Tax Act:

  • Life Insurance Premium: Deduct up to NPR 40,000 annually.
  • Health Insurance Premium: Deduct up to NPR 20,000 annually.
  • Remote Area Allowances: Flat deductions based on region category (Category A: 50k, B: 40k, C: 30k, D: 20k, E: 10k).
  • Medical Credit: Tax credit of 15% of approved medical expenses, capped at NPR 1,500.
  • Female Rebate: 10% discount on final calculated tax liability if income is solely from employment.

PWA & Interactive Visuals

Built with premium design aesthetics and user convenience in mind:

  • Progressive Web App (PWA): Fully installable on iOS, Android, and Desktop. Offline-ready and caches assets for instant load.
  • Responsive Layouts: Optimizes for mobile viewports, side-by-side comparison, or single-year separate views.
  • Dynamic SVG Charts: Circular donut visualizations showing a exact breakdown of where every rupee goes.
  • Cost to Company (CTC): Shows employer match and total package size.

Responsible AI Manifesto

A reminder to developers and creators on utilizing Artificial Intelligence to solve unique, unsolved problems rather than rebuilding existing commodities.

lightbulb

Build What Doesn't Exist

Artificial Intelligence represents a monumental leap in human productivity. However, with great power comes the responsibility of intent. Recreating standard, commoditized tools (like generic calculators, basic todo lists, or standard templates) wastes energy and human potential.

AI is best utilized to build custom, tailored solutions to localized problems that have no existing pre-packaged alternatives.

explore

Case Study: Nepal Tax Compare

This calculator is a prime example of the "Build What Doesn't Exist" mandate. While standard global tax calculators exist, none addressed the complex, concurrent, side-by-side transition from the dual tax slabs of FY 2082/83 to the new unified tax structure of FY 2083/84 in Nepal, accounting for specialized local deductions like SSF tax exemptions, remote allowances, and CIT limits in an offline-ready PWA.

We built this to solve a specific, unmet localized need.

eco

The Environmental & Cognitive Cost

Every AI generation consumes computational cycles, electrical power, and water for cooling data centers. Using these immense resources to duplicate existing open-source code or rebuild wheels contributes to carbon emissions without adding novel utility to the digital ecosystem.

Innovate responsibly. Dedicate GPU hours to original thoughts and bridging functional gaps.

verified

The AI Creator's Checklist

  • Search First: Verify if a high-quality, open-source tool already satisfies the requirement.
  • Target Gaps: Identify where existing solutions fail—due to lack of localization, poor user experience, or static limitations.
  • Augment, Don't Clone: If you must build in an existing space, add substantial new value (e.g. offline PWA capabilities, interactive visualizations, and side-by-side comparisons).
